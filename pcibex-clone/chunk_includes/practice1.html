Instructions (1/3)

How to Read

Welcome! In this experiment, you'll be reading English sentences and answering a question to probe your understanding of the sentence.

You are going to read these sentences word by word, at your own pace. Each successive word in the sentence will be revealed as you press the spacebar.

This may seem a little funny at first, but as you gain experience reading this way, you will probably develop a pace that makes the sentences understandable and roughly approximates your natural reading rate. You'll get a chance to practice after these instruction screens.

Before each sentence, you will see a dash ("—") to indicate that you may begin a sentence. When you see the dash, get ready to read a sentence then press the spacebar to begin.

Confirm your understanding
1. When will new words appear?
2. How do you start a sentence?
