Welcome!

You are invited to take part in a research study conducted by Matthew Wagers and his research associates at the University of California, Santa Cruz. Before you decide whether or not to participate in the study, you should read the information below.

Request for Consent

I understand the information given below and I voluntarily agree to participate in the research described.

Purpose. The purpose of the study is to learn about the relationship between language and other cognitive abilities.

What will happen. If you decide to take part in this study, you will be asked to:

  1. Read sentences on a computer monitor and respond to questions about them.
  2. Answer questions about your experience in the study.
It is expected your participation will take between 25 and 40 minutes.

Risks or discomfort. There are no anticipated risks in this study.

Benefits. Although you will receive no direct benefits for participation in this study (other than compensation), it may improve your understanding of how knowledge is discovered in linguistics or psychology and help the investigator better understand how individuals understand language.

Compensation. You will receive compensation via the Prolific Academic platform at the specified rate for 40 minutes of work (even if you finish in under 40 minutes).

Confidentiality. Confidentiality of your research records will be strictly maintained. Response data may be deposited in a public data repository in aggregate form. Your data files will be referenced only by a code number. No records will be kept that are linked to your personal identity. If you wish, you may request that your responses be destroyed or not used.

Decision to quit. The decision to take part in this study is completely voluntary. You do not have to participate. Even if you decide at first to take part, you are free to change your mind at any time and quit the study. You will receive compensation only if you complete the survey, however.
